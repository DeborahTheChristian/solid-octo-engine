About Me

Hello, I am Deborah, the founder of this website. Welcome to EdgyJump, a place where you can find the answers to commonly asked questions about jump roping. Over the course of 5 years, I have learnt a lot about the sport. I was first introduced to it at my primary school; I found a skipping rope, saw others having fun whilst 'skipping', and decided to join in on the fun! Ever since then, I have been using my skipping rope a lot, but now, I have decided that I would skip mainly in hot, humid weather (in the summer). Up to this day, I still find it enjoyable and hope that you will find a new hobby in it too!

Why Do I still jump rope?

  1. I like that it is an easy game to play
  2. My skipping rope is easy to carry
  3. It can be played all year round
  4. It can be played alone, with a friend or in a group
